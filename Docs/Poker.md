
# Poker

Pistoleros of Santa Rose has a way to gamble through Poker. Poker variation used is Texas hold 'em.

## Rules

- Four player maximum
- Max bets based on progress in story
- First player to go changes clockwise
- Maybe if player wins all money from other players the players reset when leaving town?

## Game loop

1. Deck is shuffled
2. All players get dealt two cards
3. Three cards placed facing up on table
4. Players Pass, Raise, Call, or Fold
5. Additional card is placed facing up on the table
6. Players Pass, Raise, Call, or Fold
7. One last card is placed facing up on the table
8. Players Pass, Raise, Call, or Fold
9. Hands are shown and counted
10. Winnings are given to the highest hand

- All players must Call or Fold on Raise, or Raise themselves
    - If all players Pass, no Call or Fold is needed
- If even hands, winnings are split
